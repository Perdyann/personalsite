Project Info

This was a web development Project for a recruitment, education services firm, that assisted job seekers in finding jobs, employers in finding the right person to hire, prospective students in studying the right course in the right university, amongst others.

Job seekers, employers and prospective students could upload/download documents such as resume, have recommendations made through the systems artificial intelligence, and view/post jobs.
